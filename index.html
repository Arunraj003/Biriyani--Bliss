Customers about Our Foods

Chicken noodles

Chicken noodles is a popular and flavorful dish that combines succulent pieces of chicken with cooked noodles, vegetables, and a delicious blend of savory sauces and spices. This dish is known for its versatility, as it can be prepared in various styles and with different types of noodles.

Mutton Biriyani

Mutton biryani is a classic and aromatic Indian dish that brings together tender mutton (goat meat) with fragrant basmati rice, spices, and herbs. This flavorful one-pot dish is known for its rich taste, distinct layers, and the perfect blend of spices.

Chicken tandoori

Chicken Tandoori is a popular and flavorful Indian dish known for its vibrant colors, aromatic spices, and smoky taste. This dish is traditionally cooked in a tandoor, a cylindrical clay oven, which imparts a unique charred and smoky flavor to the chicken.
